Welcome to the Hytale Tracker!

This website is a one-stop shop for all Hytale news: official announcements, tweets from a developer, or notable community information - it can all be found here.

The Hytale Tracker team manually sources and summarizes the news from each day, capturing notable information and important news from relevant sources.

The website itself was fully developed by That One Axolotl, and information is submitted by both Axolotl and Sophie Stone.

Please submit any issues here: https://github.com/HytaleTracker/HytaleTracker.github.io/issues
